Egg Tart

The Hong Kong egg tart, also known as “daan6 taat1,” is inspired by both British custard tarts and Portuguese pastel de nata. It features a delicate pastry base filled with silky egg custard. It is commonly served warm and is a must-have in local bakeries and dim sum.
