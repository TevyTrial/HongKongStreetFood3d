Milk Tea

Hong Kong-style milk tea, also known as “naai5 caa4,”is made from a blend of strong black teas filtered through a cloth bag, creating a smooth and rich flavour. Mixed with evaporated milk, it has a distinctive taste that reflects the city’s East-meets-West culture.
